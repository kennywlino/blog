# Intro

Working in natural language processing (NLP), I've learned a lot about how technology can interact with language to create some of the world's leading products like Siri, Alexa, or Google Translate.

When I tell people what my area of expertise is, I often hear statements like, "Oh, that sounds complicated", or "I could never do that". A lot of people talk about how programming seems too difficult or how anything STEM just scares them.

BUT I think the same could be said for any area of expertise! I mean, I still take my car to the shop for an oil change and they say that's pretty easy :eyes:

Much like working on a car or cooking in the kitchen, machine learning comes with a bunch of tools ready to make your life a lot easier. 

The same way chefs specialize in taking ingredients and cooking dishes, data scientists specialize in taking data and building ML models.
